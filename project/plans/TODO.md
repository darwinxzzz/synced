## Reference — filing completed plans

When a plan is finished and should be archived:

1. Move it from `project/plans/` or `project/build/` to `project/plans-completed/<category>/` (`implementation`, `investigation`, `migration`, `review`, or `tooling`).
2. Add a row at the top of `project/plans-completed/index.md`.
3. Add a row at the top of `project/changelog/index.md` with `Type=plan` and File `../plans-completed/<category>/<filename>`.

If explicitly requested, archive the plan under `project/changelog/plans/` instead and use `plans/<filename>` in the changelog index.
